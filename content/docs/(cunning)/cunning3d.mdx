<Lang lang="en">

**Cunning3D** is a **Native Rust DCC** built to challenge the limits of procedural modeling performance.

More than just an editor, it is a modern **high-performance geometry computing platform**. Built on **Bevy Engine** and **WGPU**, it sheds legacy debt to deliver a silky-smooth node editing experience and an industrial-grade viewport. From particles to voxels, meshes to curves, Cunning3D lets creativity flow freely in a non-destructive workflow.
